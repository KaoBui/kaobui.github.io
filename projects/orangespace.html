Brief

Help a coworking space reinforce their presence online

Orange Space is a premium coworking space in Hanoi, Vietnam. Their mission is to offer an all-in-one office space service with clean aesthetics and efficiency. They needed a website that reflected their identity as a modern, productive workspace

More than a modern website

Their request was straightforward: a beautiful website. However, as I explored their needs, I realized there was an opportunity to create something far more impactful.

The project was then about helping Orange Space build a digital identity that aligned with their core values and business goals. I then crafted a website that captured their identity at the same time seo-optimized, couppled with a SEO strategy.

My takeaways

This project taught me the importance of understanding what a client truly needs, not just what they ask for. By identifying Orange Space’s deeper goals, I was able to deliver a solution that not only met their expectations but also helped their business grow. It also showed me how understanding client needs opens the door to offering additional services that bring even greater value.
